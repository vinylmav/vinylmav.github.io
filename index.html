Bio

I'm a CS undergrad building a specialization in Audio AI — the intersection of digital signal processing, machine learning, and sound. I'm interested in how machines hear: spectral analysis, speech enhancement, source separation, and audio classification.

Currently working through a self-directed curriculum that goes from raw NumPy DSP implementations (FFT, STFT, Mel filterbanks) up through neural audio models. I like understanding things from first principles before reaching for libraries.

  • Blindsight — Chrome extension that reads and classifies Terms of Service documents using NLP, surfacing the clauses that actually matter. Built at Makeathon8 (MLSC).
